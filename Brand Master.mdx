## SECTION 10: PSYCHOLOGICAL POSITIONING

### The Buyer Psychology We Exploit (Ethically)

**Hard truth:**

SME buyers don't choose the best solution. They choose the **safest one they can defend** to their board.

### What This Means for Us

**We never lead with:**

"30% efficiency gains" → Triggers skepticism

**We lead with:**

"Here's how you defend this decision internally" → Reduces fear

**Our case studies use:**

"Fear → Relief → Pride" structure → Because that's how humans process risk

**We offer:**

30-day pilots → Not because we're generous, but because **commitment + escape route = trust**

### Core Psychological Principles

**1. Cognitive Ease**

- Simple = trustworthy

- Complex = suspicious

- Keep everything visually clean and linguistically simple

**2. Loss Aversion (Our Primary Weapon)**

- "You're losing £12K/month to manual processes" > "You could save £12K/month"

- Frame everything around what they're losing *now*

**3. Decision Fatigue**

- Provide ONE recommended path

- Remove unnecessary choices

- Make the next step obvious

**4. Trust Heuristics**

- Clean layouts = instant credibility

- Consistent colors = stability

- Premium logo = competence

**5. Social Proof**

- Mini case studies

- "Fear → Relief → Pride" testimonials

- Founder insights (not corporate claims)

### High-Converting Message Formula

1. **Identify the problem** (what's broken)

2. **Provide simple insight** (why it's broken)

3. **Give action path** (how to fix it)

4. **Show value outcome** (what improves)

**Example:**

"Your team loses 10 hours weekly to manual reporting. This happens because your tools don't talk to each other. We connect them into one system. Result: Those 10 hours go to growth, not busywork."

**The result:**

We win deals not by being the smartest option, but by being **the safe choice that happens to be smart.**

**Why:** SME buyers don't hire us for ESG compliance. They hire us to stop operational bleeding.

### Decision 3: Psychology as Competitive Edge, Not Academic Theory

**Choice:** Rewrote psychological framework as "buyer psychology we exploit."

**Why:** Listing cognitive biases = textbook. Showing how we win deals = competitive advantage.

**Why:** Personal authority beats corporate credibility for SME buyers. Ashok's pattern recognition = trust signal.
